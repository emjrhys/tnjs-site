We are TNJS

We real cool, we left school

We are a startup company that focuses on the need for shinguard security in soccer. The shinbone is the most commonly fractured long-bone in the sport and body and is very valuable to the player. So, our goal is to tend to this need by focusing on the material compositon and design of the soccer sock, implementing a pocket and an important top slot feature that allows for maximum stability of the shinguard.

What We Promise

  • Security

  • Stability

  • Service

  • Comfort
